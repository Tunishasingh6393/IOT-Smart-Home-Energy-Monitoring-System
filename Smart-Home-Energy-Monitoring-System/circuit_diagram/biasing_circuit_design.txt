========================================================================
SCT-013 ANALOG FRONT-END (AFE) BIASING CIRCUIT SPECIFICATION
========================================================================
This document diagrams and reviews the electronic conditioning circuitry 
required to interface an SCT-013 alternating current (AC) sensor clamp 
with single-ended analog-to-digital converter (ADC) microcontroller nodes.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. THE CORE PROBLEM DEFINITION
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- AC current lines generate alternating ±1.0V Peak signals across load 
  burden resistors inside the SCT-013-050 clamp.
- The standard ESP32 ADC is single-ended, capable of processing voltages 
  exclusively between 0.0V and 3.3V relative to Ground (GND).
- Any positive AC voltage above 3.3V clips signals and causes distortion.
- Any negative voltage swing (below 0V GND) sent directly to the pin clips
  at the lower limit, resulting in severe math errors. It can also physically
  degrade the micro-controller's sensitive silicon over time via forward-bias
  conduction in the ESD clamp-down protection diodes.

3. THE PHYSICS & MATH IN BRIEF
------------------------------------------------------------------------
A. Voltage Divider Center Offset:
   V_bias = V_cc * ( R2 / (R1 + R2) )
   V_bias = 3.3V * ( 10,000 / (10,000 + 10,000) ) 
   V_bias = 1.65 Volts

B. Decoupling Shunt Capacitor (10uF):
   Fills or sucks current variations at 50Hz/60Hz mains, maintaining 
   an extremely stable 1.65V reference node even when the mains surge.

C. Combined Wave Expression:
   V_adc(t) = V_bias + V_clamp(t)
   V_adc(t) = 1.65V + 1.0V * sin(omega * t)
   V_max = 1.6 center + 1.0V Peak = 2.65 Volts (Fully Safe < 3.3V Limit)
   V_min = 1.6 center - 1.0V Peak = 0.65 Volts (Fully Safe > 0.0V Ground)

------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. RECOMMENDED COMPONENT BILL-OF-MATERIALS (BOM)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
- 2x  10k Ohm Resistors (1% Metal Film tolerance for precise bias center)
- 1x  120 Ohm Resistor (Burden, needed if using current-type SCT-013-000)
- 1x  10 uF Electrolytic Capacitor (voltage rating >= 10V)
- 1x  100 nF Ceramic Capacitor (bypass high-frequency grid noise harmonics)
- 1x  ESP32 DevKitC Node or compatible board
- 1x  SCT-013-050 Non-Invasive CT Sensor Clamp (Internal burden included)
